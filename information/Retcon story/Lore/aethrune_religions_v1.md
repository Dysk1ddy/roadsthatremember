# AETHRUNE — RELIGIONS & BELIEF SYSTEMS (V1)

## PURPOSE
Define the major belief systems of Aethrune.
- Replace traditional DnD deity structures
- Align with themes of system, signal, memory, and survival
- Provide narrative hooks for NPCs, factions, and quests

---

# DESIGN PRINCIPLE

Religion in Aethrune is NOT purely divine.

It is:
- interpretations of the Meridian systems
- cultural responses to collapse
- attempts to explain signal, memory, and control

Deities may be:
- real
- misunderstood systems
- symbolic constructs
- or something deeper

---

# 1. THE LANTERN FAITH

## Core Belief
“Light is not truth. Light is what lets us endure truth.”

## Deity / Entity
**The First Flame**

## Nature
- Possibly real
- Possibly symbolic
- Represents endurance, warmth, and human connection

## Practices
- Lighting lanterns for the lost
- Naming the dead to preserve identity
- Community gatherings at Lantern Posts

## Followers
- Elira Dawnmantle
- civilians of Iron Hollow

## Themes
- compassion
- memory
- survival through connection

---

# 2. THE QUIET CHOIR

## Core Belief
“The world is a pattern. We exist to hear it.”

## Deity / Entity
**The Listening Vast**

## Nature
- Likely real
- Exists beyond comprehension
- Interprets existence as data

## Practices
- silence rituals
- signal attunement
- controlled thought discipline

## Followers
- Caldra Voss
- Choir agents

## Themes
- control
- inevitability
- surrender to system truth

---

# 3. THE MERIDIAN DOCTRINE

## Core Belief
“The system was built. Therefore it can be understood.”

## Deity / Entity
None (strictly non-theistic)

## Nature
- Rejects worship
- Treats Meridian systems as engineered truth

## Practices
- excavation
- study
- reconstruction

## Followers
- Nim Ardentglass (partial alignment)
- Reclaimers

## Themes
- knowledge
- control through understanding
- rationalism

---

# 8. THE FRACTURED SIGNAL (HERETICAL)

## Core Belief
“The system is broken. We must break it further.”

## Deity / Entity

## Nature

## Practices

- signal disruption
- chaos rituals

## Followers

- extremists

## Themes

- rebellion
- destruction of control

---

# RELIGION INTERACTIONS

## Major Conflicts
- Lantern Faith vs Quiet Choir (humanity vs system)
- Meridian Doctrine vs all faiths (logic vs belief)
- Ashen Creed vs Free Path (control vs autonomy)
- Quiet Choir vs Fractured Signal (order vs chaos)
