# Enhanced RPG Leveling & Ability System

## 🎯 Core Concept
Players gain abilities through **choice-based progression**, not fixed unlocks. This increases build diversity, replayability, and player agency.

---

## 🧠 Ability Structure

Each character has:
- **Active Abilities:** 4–6 equipped
- **Passive Traits:** 2–4 equipped
- **Core Skills:** Basic actions (always available)

---

## 🔓 Level Progression (1–10)

### Level 1 — Base Class
- Choose: Warrior / Mage / Rogue  
- Gain:
  - 2 core abilities  
  - Choose 1 ability (Tier 1)

---

### Level 2 — Early Customization
- Choose:
  - 1 ability (Tier 1 or utility)
  - 1 passive trait

---

### Level 3 — Subclass Unlock
- Choose subclass  
- Gain:
  - 1 signature ability  
  - Choose 1 subclass ability (Tier 2)

---

### Level 4 — Build Definition
- Choose:
  - 1 ability (Class or Subclass)
  - Stat increase OR Feat

---

### Level 5 — Power Spike
- Choose:
  - 1 strong ability (Tier 2/3)
- Upgrade:
  - 1 existing ability

---

### Level 6 — Utility Layer
- Choose:
  - 1 utility ability
  - 1 passive trait

---

### Level 7 — Subclass Specialization
- Choose:
  - 1 subclass upgrade path

---

### Level 8 — Major Customization
- Choose:
  - 1 ability (any tier)
  - Stat increase OR Feat

---

### Level 9 — High Impact Choice
- Choose:
  - 1 advanced ability (near-ultimate)
- Upgrade:
  - 1 ability to Mastered

---

### Level 10 — Capstone
- Choose:
  - 1 Capstone Ability
- Gain:
  - Final defining passive

---

## 🧩 Ability Tiers

### Tier 1 (Levels 1–3)
- Basic, reliable, low cost

### Tier 2 (Levels 3–6)
- Specialized, synergy-based

### Tier 3 (Levels 5–8)
- Strong, build-defining

### Tier 4 (Levels 9–10)
- High-impact, near-ultimate

---

## ⚙️ Loadout System
- 4 Active Abilities early → 6 max  
- 2 Passives early → 4 max  

---

## 🔁 Ability Upgrades
Abilities can evolve instead of being replaced.

Example:
- Strike →
  - Bleed Strike
  - Cleaving Strike
  - Lifesteal Strike

---

## 🧠 Passive Trait Examples
- Bonus damage vs poisoned targets  
- Gain shield at low HP  
- Restore resource on kill  
- Increased crit after dodge  

---

## 🔥 Subclass Modification System

### Poisoner Paths
- Burst detonation  
- Long-term damage over time  

### Juggernaut Paths
- Pure tank  
- Reflect damage  

---

## ⚖️ System Benefits
- Frequent meaningful choices  
- High replayability  
- Clear structure for text-based gameplay  
- Modernized DnD-style progression
