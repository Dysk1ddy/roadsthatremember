# AGENTS.md

## Repo-Wide Writing Instructions

Use this instruction set for lore docs, scene drafts, dialogue notes, story summaries, planning docs, and AI-assisted writing work across this repo unless the user asks for a different style.

## Hard Rules

- Do not use the contrastive thesis pattern "it is not X, it is Y".
- Avoid close variants such as `not just`, `not merely`, `not only`, `in truth`, `at its core`, and `the real`.
- Do not build suspense around a definition when a direct statement would do the job better.

## Preferred Style

- Replace thesis sentences with direct statements. Say what the thing is immediately, then add a concrete detail or consequence.
- Cut `overview`, `core identity`, and `importance` scaffolding in lore docs. Keep lists for mechanics, flags, and implementation. Use prose for setting voice.
- Reduce abstract emphasis words such as `crucial`, `key`, `significant`, `important`, `deeper`, `broader`, `landscape`, `underscores`, and `highlights`.
- Break the symmetry. Use fewer mirrored lines, fewer rule-of-three lists, and fewer clean oppositions.
- Lead with specific physical detail. Prefer concrete nouns over abstractions: `winch`, `mud`, `ledger`, `quarantine rope`, `ash-bitter poison`.
- Keep one sentence per section that sounds like a person noticed it, not like a model summarized it.

## Sentence Patterns To Prefer

- Direct: `Greywake is a survey outpost built to certify routes.`
- Historical: `Greywake started as a survey camp and kept growing around the work.`
- Observer lens: `Travelers call it a town; the clerks run it like a ledger with walls.`
- Cause and effect: `Because Greywake certifies routes, every faction wants a hand on its books.`

## Human Edit Pass

After drafting:

- Cut any sentence that could fit any fantasy setting.
- Replace it with one weird local detail, one human motive, or one concrete consequence.
- Turn bullet stacks into short paragraphs where voice matters.
- Search for and rewrite: `not just`, `not merely`, `not only`, `in truth`, `at its core`, `the real`.
